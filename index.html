✅All vegetables except large amounts of spinach (oxalate)

🚫Refined sugar — sodas, sweets, packaged snacks, white sugar
🚫Yeast foods — commercial bread, pizza dough, fermented items
🚫High sodium — fast food, packet noodles, canned soups, processed meats
🚫Large amounts of spinach, peanuts, almonds — high oxalate (kidney stones)
⚠️Nightshades (tomatoes, eggplant, peppers) — test carefully. If eating them triggers flares, eliminate.

💊 Your Stack
🥚 Egg White Protein Powder
1–2 scoops/day in water
Essential
Primary protein supplement. Dairy-free, easy to digest, neutral taste — much better than pea protein. Mix with water only. Brands: NOW Sports, Jay Robb, or MyProtein. Can order via iHerb if not available locally in Qatar.
🐟 Omega-3 Fish Oil
2g EPA+DHA daily with a meal
Essential
Directly reduces systemic inflammation — one of the best things you can do for HS alongside diet. Also improves fat metabolism. Take with food to avoid aftertaste.
☀️ Vitamin D3 + K2
3000–4000 IU D3 daily
Essential
Most people in Qatar working indoors are deficient. D3 is anti-inflammatory and helps immune regulation — key for HS. K2 ensures calcium goes to your bones, not your kidneys (critical for your stone history).
🌙 Magnesium Glycinate
300mg before bed
Recommended
Improves sleep quality (you need to get from 6–7 hrs to 7.5+), reduces inflammation, and speeds muscle recovery. Glycinate form is gentle on stomach. Take 30 min before sleep.
🔷 Zinc
30mg/day — max 3 months then reassess
HS Specific
Clinical studies show zinc reduces HS flare frequency and severity. Take with food to avoid nausea. Don't run it indefinitely as it can deplete copper — take a break after 3 months.
⚡ Creatine Monohydrate
5g/day — DOCTOR CLEARANCE FIRST
⚠️ Check First
Very effective for strength and body composition. However, with your kidney stone history, you MUST drink minimum 4L water daily when taking it and get your nephrologist's OK first. It raises creatinine levels which looks alarming on routine tests.

🔴 HS + Weight Loss: Losing weight is THE most effective intervention for HS severity. Every kilogram you lose reduces friction, sweating, and systemic inflammation. This plan is designed to make that happen as efficiently as possible.
⚕️ HS Rules
🔵Shower immediately after every single workout — no exceptions
🔵Wear loose, moisture-wicking gym clothes — nothing tight in problem areas
🔵Use gentle, fragrance-free soap on affected areas post-workout
🔵Keep a food journal — note which specific foods trigger flares for you personally
🔵Test nightshades separately: eliminate tomatoes, eggplant, peppers for 3 weeks and see if flares reduce
🔵Anti-inflammatory eating is your long-term weapon: fatty fish, turmeric, ginger, olive oil
🫘 Kidney Stone Rules
🟡4 litres of water daily — this is your #1 prevention strategy, non-negotiable
🟡Squeeze lemon into your water — citrate prevents calcium oxalate stone formation
🟡Spread your protein intake across all meals — don't overload in one sitting
🟡Keep sodium very low — fast food, salty snacks, and processed meats all increase stone risk
🟡No high-dose Vitamin C supplements — converts to oxalate in your body
🟡Consult your doctor before starting creatine — requires extra hydration and medical clearance
🧠 Mindset & Recovery
⚡Sleep 7.5 hrs minimum — you're at 6–7 hrs now. Poor sleep spikes cortisol which literally holds belly fat.
⚡Walk on rest days — 20–30 min of walking burns additional calories and reduces inflammation
⚡Add weight or reps every 1–2 weeks — progressive overload is what drives body change
⚡Expect 0.5–1kg loss per week — faster = muscle loss. Slow and steady is the strategy.
⚡Take monthly photos, not daily scale readings — weight fluctuates ±2kg from water alone
⚡Recalculate your calorie target every 4 weeks — as you lose weight, your TDEE drops too
